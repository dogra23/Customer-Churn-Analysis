# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy
---


## *Problem Statement*
### The telecom company is experiencing a high churn rate leading to significant revenue loss. The objective of this project is to identify key drivers of churn, analyze high-risk customer segments, and provide actionable retention strategies.
---

## *Tools Used*
- SQL (Data Analysis)
- Power BI (Dashboard & visualization)
- Excel (Data Cleaning)
- Python
---


## *Key Insights*
- Month-to-month customers show the highest churn rate (42.7%)
- Customers within first 6 months churn at 53%
- Fiber optic customers have the highest churn (40.7%)
- Customers without tech support churn 2x more
- Competitor offers are the leading cause of churn
---

## *Business Impact*
- Monthly Revenue Loss: $139K
- Total Revenue Loss: $2.8M
- Identified high-risk customer segments for targeted retention
---


## *Recommedations*
- Promote long-term contracts
- Improve onboarding for new customers
- Bundle technical support services
- Introduce retention offers for high-risk customers
